Privacy Policy

CookiePopper is designed with privacy in mind. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the extension to function properly:

Financial and Payment Information

  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe (PCI compliant)
  • We do not store credit card numbers or payment details
  • Subscription status is stored locally on your device
  • Payment history is managed by Stripe, not by us

Website Content

  • Extension scans webpage content locally to detect cookie banners
  • No webpage content is stored, transmitted, or collected
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • We only look for cookie banner elements, not your personal data

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Health information
  • Authentication information (passwords, credentials)
  • Personal communications
  • Location data (GPS, IP address)
  • Web browsing history
  • User activity monitoring (clicks, keystrokes)
  • Search queries or browsing patterns
  • Device information beyond basic extension functionality

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No tracking or analytics of user behavior
  • No user profiling or behavioral analysis
  • Extension settings are saved locally in your browser

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide cookie banner dismissal functionality
  • To manage your subscription status (stored locally)
  • To improve extension performance
  • To provide customer support when requested

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe (PCI compliant)
  • We may share data only if required by law
  • No data is shared for marketing purposes

Your Rights

  • Access: Your data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • Opt out: Disable the extension to stop data collection
  • Portability: Data is stored in standard browser storage
  • Correction: Update settings through the extension interface

Security

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • No data transmission means no interception risk
  • Extension uses standard browser security features
  • Payment processing uses Stripe's secure infrastructure

Compliance

  • GDPR compliant (European Union)
  • CCPA compliant (California)
  • Chrome Web Store privacy requirements
  • Open source code for transparency

Children's Privacy

CookiePopper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or our website.
